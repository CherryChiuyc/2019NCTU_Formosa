High School Workshop

   With the heart of spreading knowledge, we hold few synthetic biology mini-speeches and a science camp for high school students. By engaging with students who have passions in science, we received much valuable feedback about the project. The workshop seemed to pique an interest in synthetic biology in many of the students, and we sincerely hope they decide to continue pursuing this passion.

   This conference was sat in the main hall of the NTHU library. It is a public place that anyone could walk by and join. Taiwan iGEM Conference

   NCTU Formosa iGEM team took a short trip to Taichung, a significant city in mid Taiwan. The NCHU hold the biggest iGEM conference this year, all the teams in Taiwan are invited. Over seven other teams attended the event, and each team took the opportunity to share their projects. We received much constructive criticism from not only other students but teachers and advisors as well. From this, we were able to improve both our content as well as our presentation style. Additionally, we bonded with many other teams and schools and made long-lasting friendships.

Figure 4: The poster section in Taiwan iGEM conference

NCTU Annual Biotechnology Fair

   This is the annual biotechnology fair for incoming candidates of the biological science and technology department in NCTU. Our team members set an information table about iGEM and synthetic biology for the students who are interested in science. We also invited them to join the iGEM competition for next year.
